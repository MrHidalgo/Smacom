SmaCom™ are natural high quality frozen semi-finished items.

SmaCom™ entered the market in October 2010. The new brand implies innovation in everything: quality, taste, production - innovations with a consistently high level. "SmaCom" always offers the most advanced, the most delicious, the best on the market of semis. The brand was created for those who seek for a better, for those who appreciate delicious food and their own time.

The brand name "SmaCom" characterizes the products and the company's mission: first, high-quality products with excellent taste properties, and secondly, a harmonious synthesis of Ukrainian taste traditions and European production levels.
