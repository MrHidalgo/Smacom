Pancakes

with chicken

TM SmaCom presents a new dish which you will remember as the most unforgettable culinary trip in your life. Meet the pancakes with chicken! This is absolutely both ready-to-eat and unique product because of its ingredients.

We are sure you will fall in love in them from first sight! The pancake is like a gold lace combined with juicy chicken and fried onions. They absorbed all flavors and the taste of delicacy filling.

The pancakes are very easy to cook. You will find our product on the supermarket’s shelves in ultramodern packages. Live without troubles and enjoy your life!

Ingredients
Dough: water, wheat flour, sugar, egg powder, refined deodorized sunflower oil, salt, , food soda, citric acid. Filling: chicken meat raw cooked 75% (poultry chicken – 45%, chicken fillet – 30%), wilted onion, water, salt, black pepper powder.

Cook Method

Put pancakes into already warmed and oiled pan. Fry on low heat, gradually turning, until a ruddy crust is formed on all sides.
