Pancakes

with cheese and raisins

New pancakes with cheese and raisings are the combination of exquisite taste, nutritional value and attractive aristocratic food. We offer a new desert with exquisite filling: cheese and raisins. The combination of these ingredients known since childhood.

For those who are fond of sweets, pancakes are the perfect dish. The new cooking method is a peculiarity of pancakes. We fry the dough on both sides until delicate pattern and gold crust.

You will be excited by taste and charming modern packaging. Enjoy your meal!

Ingredients
Dough: water, wheat flour, sugar, refined deodorized sunflower oil, salt, egg powder, food soda, citric acid. Filling: cottage cheese – 74%, raisins – 17%, sugar, salt, vanillin.

Cook Method

Put pancakes into already warmed and oiled pan. Fry on low heat, gradually turning, until a ruddy crust is formed on all sides.
