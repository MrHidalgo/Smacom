Pancakes

with strawberry

Love to sweets, possibly, can be compared with people’s love. TM SmaCom cooked a new dessert and put all tenderness and the taste of strawberry in it. Meet the pancakes with strawberry!

We wrapped whole pieces of juicy berries in thin lace dough, cooked according a new recipe. The new cooking method is a peculiarity of pancakes. We fry the dough on both sides until delicate pattern and gold crust.

The dish is served in 5 minutes. This is ready dessert which you need only to warm up. Enjoy the source of vitamins!

Ingredients
Dough: water, wheat flour, sugar, refined deodorized sunflower oil, salt, egg powder, food soda, citric acid. Filling: strawberries, white sugar, water, potato starch.

Cook Method

Put pancakes into already warmed and oiled pan. Fry on low heat, gradually turning, until a ruddy crust is formed on all sides.
