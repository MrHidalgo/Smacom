Natural ingredients and innovative freezing allow us to store all nutritious value and taste. You will find our product on the supermarket’s shelves in ultramodern colorful packages. Live without troubles and enjoy your life!

Ingredients
Dough, potable water, top-grade flour, white sugar, egg powder, deodorized sunflower oil, table salt, soda, citric acid. Filling: boiled meat 80% (beef 60%, pork 20%), stir-fried onions, potable water, edible table salt, ground pepper.

Cook Method

Put pancakes into already warmed and oiled pan. Fry on low heat, gradually turning, until a ruddy crust is formed on all sides.
