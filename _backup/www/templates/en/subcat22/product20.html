Pork stew

Pork Stew is very tender, with a pleasant slightly sweet taste.

It’s impossible to count a great number of meals that can be prepared from beef, but the most popular and favorite one has always been and remained a stew. Besides stewing is considered as one of the most healthiest methods of cooking meat. It allows you to save a large amount of nutrients and vitamins that remain in a ready meal, and not to pour off water (broth), like during cooking.

We had an important task is to make beef soft, juicy, tender. And we did it! Appreaciate the taste of beef stew from TM SmaCom!

Cook Method

How to warm up meals:

- warm up the meal components, submerging it in hot water;
- warm up the meal components, placing it on any heater (convection heater, radiator, etc.);
- warm up the components over a fire, previously placed them in the pot (saucepan, frying pan, etc.);

After warming open carefully the retort pouch (especially the first course) with a help of a tear strip and put into a dish.


Stir the meal and eat. A package with a second meal (porridge) is recommended before opening to mash in your hands and then open it with a tear strip. Stir the food and eat.
