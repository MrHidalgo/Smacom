Borshch with chicken/pork/beef

Ukrainian borshch is a dish that has made Ukrainian cuisine famous. The smell and taste of any Ukrainian borshch is unmistakable and incomparable. Ukrainians are proud of their special meal.

TM "SmaCom" represents a real Ukrainian borshch of three types depending on the meat stock: borshch with chicken, pork and beef.

A rich, delicious, nutritious and most importantly – it is natural! The cooks of TM «SmaSom» follow the recipe of Ukrainian cuisine. Ukrainian borshch is the source of many vitamins for human health, because all the selected vegetables are only stewed and boiled, preserving nutrients.

Cook Method

How to warm up meals:

- warm up the meal components, submerging it in hot water;
- warm up the meal components, placing it on any heater (convection heater, radiator, etc.);
- warm up the components over a fire, previously placed them in the pot (saucepan, frying pan, etc.);

After warming open carefully the retort pouch (especially the first course) with a help of a tear strip and put into a dish.


Stir the meal and eat. A package with a second meal (porridge) is recommended before opening to mash in your hands and then open it with a tear strip. Stir the food and eat.
