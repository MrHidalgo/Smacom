Ukrainian Borshch with beans and beef

TM Soup has a range of broth-based red borshch. In addition, we offered another version of this wonderful meal - borshch with beans. Unlike the classic borshch, in this recipe there are beans, which make the first meal more richer and tastier.

To get the light and healthy meal we prepare borshch with beans and beef.

Ingredients
Мясо (говядина, свинина, курица), фасоль, картофель, капуста белокочанная, свекла красная, лук репчатый, морковь, масло подсолнечное рафинированное дезодорированное, паста томатная, соль поваренная пищевая, сахар, перец сладкий, уксус, перец черный молотый

Cook Method

How to warm up meals:

- warm up the meal components, submerging it in hot water;
- warm up the meal components, placing it on any heater (convection heater, radiator, etc.);
- warm up the components over a fire, previously placed them in the pot (saucepan, frying pan, etc.);

After warming open carefully the retort pouch (especially the first course) with a help of a tear strip and put into a dish.


Stir the meal and eat. A package with a second meal (porridge) is recommended before opening to mash in your hands and then open it with a tear strip. Stir the food and eat.
