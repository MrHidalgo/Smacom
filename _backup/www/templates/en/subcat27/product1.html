Chevupizza

Pizza is one of the favourite food of both adults and children. Why that? Because it is a combination of crispy dough and delicious filling. We decided to make a unique culinary delight out of ordinary pizza.

The secret of chevupizza in its filling and form. The main ingredients: cheese “Mozzarella”, sausage and Bulgarian pepper which go well with tender cheburek. Chevupizza is ready to eat. Warm it up for 5 minutes in microwave oven and you will get a delicious rich dish.

Ingredients
Top-grade flour, potable water, deodorized-sunflower oil, edible table salt, white sugar, acetic acid.
Filling: boiled sausage 36% (greasy pork, potable water, mechanically deboned poultry, chicken skin, protein stabilizer, wheat flour, semolina, edible salt, powdered milk, nutritional supplements), smoked sausage 22% (mechanically deboned poultry, chicken skin, potable water, lard, protein stabilizer, edible table salt, potato powder, nutritional supplements), cheese “Mozzarella” 11% (cow’s milk, edible table salt, calcium chloride, milk ferment leaven, milk- clotting enzymes), tomato paste (tomato, preservative – sodium benzoate, citric acid) , potable water, Bulgarian pepper, white sugar, edible salt, oregano.
