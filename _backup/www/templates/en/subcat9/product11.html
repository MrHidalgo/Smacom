Oily, homogeneous cottage cheese is among the best examples of home cooking and dairy products. It is highly enjoyable in appearance and taste. Knowledge and care of our professionals create high-quality sweet cottage cheese (high quality cottage cheese is never sour). Adding raisins to the pancakes gives firstly, even more useful microelements, and, secondly, is a real treat for true lovers of sweets. The combination of cottage cheese and raisins is familiar to everyone from their childhood. For everyone who loves a sweet treat, pancakes with cottage cheese and raisins is the perfect dish.

Ingredients
dough: water, extra class wheat flour, milk powder, sugar, sunflower oil, salt, powdered eggs.
Filling: cottage cheese, raisins - 16%, sugar, drinking water, semolina, salt, vanilla flavoring.
