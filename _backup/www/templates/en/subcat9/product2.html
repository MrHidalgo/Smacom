Meat dumplings

with pork and beef

SmaCom™ is ready to offer you a decent solution - meat dumplings for the whole family. Meat dumplings with pork and beef have a favorable appetizing "Saturn" shape that provides a higher ratio of meat to dough.

The particular feature is filling of a harmonious combination of pork, beef and onions. Everything is observed in certain proportions. Pork makes the meat dumplings tender, beef makes them nourishing, onions make them juicy, and allspice and black pepper create a delicious spicy flavor of the dish.

Wherever you are - at work or on holiday, you can always cook the meat dumplings and enjoy their taste!

Ingredients
Dough: extra class flour, water, salt
Minced meat: pork - 42%, fat - 19%, beef - 14%, onion, water, wheat fiber, salt, ground black pepper.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the meat dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of meat dumplings to 4 parts of water). Bring to a boil, cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat until cooked. Serve the meat dumplings with butter, sour-cream or sauce.
