Meat Dumplings

with pork

SmaCom™ proudly presents meat dumplings with pork. Only natural products are used, the ratio of meat to dough is 50/50, thanks to a successful Saturn shape. The meat dumplings consist of fat juicy pork and, of course, of gentle dough from high quality flour. Real home-grown onion gives the SmaCom™ meat dumplings with pork special juiciness, and natural spices emphasize a sophisticated flavor palette.

Meat dumplings with pork have a pleasant spicy flavor, they are nourishing and juicy.

Enjoy your meal!

Ingredients
Dough: extra class flour, water, salt
Minced meat: pork - 55%, lard - 20%, onion, water, wheat fiber, salt, ground black pepper.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the meat dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of meat dumplings to 4 parts of water). Bring to a boil, cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat until cooked. Serve the meat dumplings with butter, sour-cream or sauce.
