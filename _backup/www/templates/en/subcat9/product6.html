Thin delicate crumbly dough and golden potatoes is the combination not only aesthetically pleasing, but also very tasty. We have embodied this particular harmony of taste into our dumplings with potatoes. This is an amazing synthesis of the potatoes Energy (caloric) 100 g, juicy onion and spices. Ingredients
Dough: Extra class flour, water, sunflower oil, egg powder, salt.
Filling: boiled potatoes, fried onion, salt, ground black pepper.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of dumplings: 4 parts of water), bring to a boil and cook over low heat for 7-10 minutes. Dumplings are served with butter and sour cream.
