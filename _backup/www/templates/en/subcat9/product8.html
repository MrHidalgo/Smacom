Dumplings

with cottage cheese
1

SmaCom™ dumplings with cottage cheese are the traditional dumplings, created for those who do not only love tasty and nourishing food, but also for those who honor dairy products and care about their health. Cottage cheese filling is an excellent source of calcium and phosphorus. It is impossible to form the skeletal system without these elements. That is why SmaCom™ dumplings with cottage cheese are useful for everyone, but especially they are recommended for children.

Dough is made from extra class flour. While cooking, it becomes gentle and lush. A special cooling technology allows to get an absolute strength and integrity of the dough. Cottage cheese for the filling is an exceptionally natural dairy product.

An elegant and delicious mix of lush dough and sweet cottage cheese is useful for children and adults. A delicate flavor of SmaCom™ dumplings with cottage cheese will leave nobody indifferent!

Ingredients
Dough: Extra class flour, water, sunflower oil, egg powder, salt.
Filling: fermented cottage cheese, semolina, sugar, salt, vanilla.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of dumplings: 4 parts of water), bring to a boil and cook over low heat for 7-10 minutes. Dumplings are served with butter and sour cream.
