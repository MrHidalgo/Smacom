Bulmens

with pork and beef

Introducing an entirely new product, not like any of the existing frozen semi-finished products on the market. It is a bulmens with pork and beef.

Bulmens is a delicate and juicy culinary masterpiece, it is irresistible to resist! The secret of bulmens is revealed during cooking, during which meat broth is formed inside of the product

We are sure that small round form of bulmens will be appeal not only to adults, but also to children. We recommend to try the bulmens with turkey, and bulmens with beef.

Ingredients
Minced meat: meat raw frozen 64.5% (pork meat is uncultivated 28.9%, beef meat is unnecessary 29.8%, pork fat 5.8%), drinking water, onion, salt, pepper black ground, glutamate sodium. Dough: wheat flour, drinking water, wheat flour, salt.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of dumplings: 4 parts of water), bring to a boil and cook over low heat for 3 minutes. Dumplings are served with butter and sour cream.
