Bulmens

with beef

TM SmaCom is keen to create special products that leave a lasting taste and a desire to try again. New line of semi-finished products - a bulmens, 100% will be remembered for a long time to everyone. Bulmens is a delicate and juicy culinary masterpiece, it is irresistible to resist!

The secret of bulmens is revealed during cooking, during which meat broth is formed inside of the product Thanks to a delicate dough during the application of a bulmens do not lose a single drop of fragrant juice.

We are sure that small round form of bulmens will be appeal not only to adults, but also to children. We recommend to try the, bulmens with pork and beef and bulmens with turkey.

Ingredients
Minced meat: 63.7% frozen meat (friable beef is 53.9%, pork fat is 9.8%), drinking water, onion, vegetable salt, ground black pepper, ground coriander, sodium glutamate. Dough: wheat flour, drinking water, wheat flour, salt.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of dumplings: 4 parts of water), bring to a boil and cook over low heat for 3 minutes. Dumplings are served with butter and sour cream.
