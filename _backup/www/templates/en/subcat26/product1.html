Bulmens

with turkey

TM SmaCom invites you to a fascinating world of bulmens with turkey! We created a unique dish that looks similar to round dumplings, but is special for its filling.

At first, stuffing. We stuff bulmens with chilled turkey meat, which retains all nutritional value. Special attention deserves a tender dough, thanks to which the product does not lose a single drop of meat juice. The secret of bulmens is revealed during cooking, during which meat broth is formed inside of the product Bulmeni is a delicate and juicy culinary masterpiece, it is irresistible to resist!

We are sure that small round form of bulmens will be appeal not only to adults, but also to children. We recommend to try the bulmens with pork and beef, and bulmens with beef.

Ingredients
Minced meat: raw meat is frozen 62.7% (turkey 35.4%, chicken thigh 27.3%), drinking water, onion, margarine, salt, cow's milk, ground chili pepper black ground, glutamate sodium.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of dumplings: 4 parts of water), bring to a boil and cook over low heat for 3 minutes. Dumplings are served with butter and sour cream.
