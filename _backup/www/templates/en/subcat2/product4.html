Ukrainian dumplings are not just a food, but a national color, symbol and pride. Appetizing crescents have been glorified in the Ukrainian literature and are loved in every house. Ukrainian cherry dumplings are the world's legend without any doubts. Any foreigner arriving in Ukraine, first of all, will want to taste the dumplings.

SmaCom™ dumplings with cherries are: molded white dough made of the finest flour and juicy cherries, carefully cleaned of stones.

Cook Method

Put cherry dumplings in a plenty of salted boiling water. Boil until cooked. Finished dumplings start to float. Remove them with a slotted spoon and pour the warmed butter on them. Sweet syrup can be served separately. The best syrup choice is cherry or black cherry syrup, you can also add jam.
