For the human daily diet first courses are a must. According to your preferences TM “Smacom” offers some range of first courses: Ukrainian borsch with meat, Ukrainian borshch with mushrooms, pea soup, vegetable soup, noodle soup, rice soup, creamy beet borshch, creamy mushroom soup, creamy spinach soup.

A wide range of first courses will impress the fussy foodies of beef broth and veggie foodies as well. Besides, the recipes of first courses are based not only on culinary traditions of Ukrainian cuisine but the peculiarities of national cuisine of other countries.

Every soup and borshch has its own cooking techniques, on which the taste and flavor depends. Due to unique freezing methods all nutrients are preserved. Frozen meals are simply cooked, just warm them up for 8-10 minutes.
