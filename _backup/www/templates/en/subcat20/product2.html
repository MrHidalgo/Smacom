Grain bun

Bakery of TM «SmaCom» is the flavor of home. After all, nothing is more synonymous with family home as the smell of fresh bread! Cooks put his heart and soul into the process of preparation of buns giving you taste and spiritual enjoyment.

TM «SmaCom» created a very useful and indispensable product on the table is a grain bun. It is difficult to overestimate the benefits of the bakery, because the grains clean the organism and contribute to body weigh management. It is recommended for people who keep up the diet.

Ingredients
First grade wheat flour, water, rye flour, compressed yeast, sugar, refined deodorized sunflower oil, salt.

Cook Method

№1: Warm up bread in a preheated oven for 3 – 4 minutes at a temperature of 190 – 210 ° C.

№2: Warm up buns in a microwave oven 3 – 4 minutes with a power of 700 watts

In both cases, the heating time depends on the number of products which are heated simultaneously, large number of patties require more time. Buns should not be defrosted before cooking.
