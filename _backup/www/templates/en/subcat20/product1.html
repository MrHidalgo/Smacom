Wheat Bun

Wheat bun of TM «SmaCom» is light, soft and very tasty! Those who does not imagine their diet without bread, the pastries of TM «SmaCom» is sure to find its place in your menu!

And if you decide to entertain guests with tasty buns, the round shape and wonderful taste will allow you to cook the most delicious hot snacks!

Ingredients
First grade wheat flour, water, compressed yeast, sugar, refined deodorized sunflower oil, salt.

Net weight and packing:
  • 1 kg
Cook Method

№1: Warm up bread in a preheated oven for 3 - 4 minutes at a temperature of 190 - 210 ° C.

№2: Warm up buns in a microwave oven 3 - 4 minutes with a power of 700 watts.

In both cases, the heating time depends on the number of products which are heated simultaneously, large number of patties require more time. Buns should not be defrosted before cooking.
