SmaCom™ proudly presents a line of high-quality frozen convenience foods.

Products are presented in a convenient package of Doy-pack type. Learn more about this innovative packaging.

Package features

Packaging is made of two-layer lamination film, which has a number of functional properties:

  • Air-tightness is provided by a strong metal clip fastener that prevents pouring out from the opened package.
  • The presence of a resistant bottom allows you to conveniently place the Doy-pack package in the refrigerators and shop-windows.
  • The new package proposes a balanced range of the most capacious categories: meat dumplings, khinkali, dumplings and pancakes.

Juicy filling and thin dough are the main distinguishing characteristics of SmaCom™ meat dumplings. Meat dumplings are made accordingly to the current DSTU standarts for the production of this product. Meat dumplings have a circular "Saturn" shape, which provides a greater value of meat to dough than the "ravioli" form.

Lush, delicious dumplings from SmaCom™ are presented with the most popular types of fillings in Ukraine. Fillings are made only from natural ingredients: natural potatoes and fresh mushrooms in the dumplings with potato filling, natural cottage cheese in the cottage cheese dumplings, frozen, crushed berries in the dumplings with cherries.

Openwork pancakes from SmaCom™ are outstanding in their production technology. It is based on a bilateral frying of dough, ie, exactly the same way of making pancakes at home. This provides an excellent taste, attractive appearance of the product and its high degree of readiness.
