Frozen yeast puff

pastry

This pastry is created for real greedy for sweets. It can be used without additional rolling for maximum raising or be rolled into a thin layer for cooking cakes, pies or pizza bases. A convenient packaging of pastry in sheets (instead of rolls) allows you to defrost only the needed amount of it.

Enjoy your pastries!

Ingredients
Extra class flour, drinking water, puff pastry margarine, natural-identical flavoring "Butter-cream", natural colorant β-carotene, citric acid, baking yeast, salt, sugar, bakery improver, egg powder, "vanilla" flavoring.

Cook Method

Release the dough from the outer packaging, thaw it at a room temperature of 20-25 °C. If necessary, roll the dough to the desired thickness. Form products, put them on a greased baking tray. For cutting, use a sharp knife or a recess, not to crush the edges of the dough. Cover with polyethylene to prevent chapping. Leave in a warm place (+25 ... +28 ° C), give time to rise. To give the finished products gloss and nice color, grease the surface with eggs before baking. Preheat oven to 220-230 ° C. Bake at 220-230 ° C until golden brown (small items - 10-15 min., large ones - 15-20 min.).
