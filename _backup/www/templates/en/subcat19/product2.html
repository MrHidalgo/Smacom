Compote

Summer is a the perfect time of vitamins and time of harvesting the nature gifts for winter. Warm time gives us a lot of delicious berries, thanks to which we can enjoy summer drinks in cold weather.

TM «SmaCom» preserved a piece of summer in the frozen juice, which vitamins help to keep the human body in winter. The drink contains cherry, black currant, strawberry and plum is a very good berry combination!

Ingredients
Drinking water, frozen mixture for compote (cherry, black currant, strawberry, plum), sugar, citric acid.

  • proteins0,1 g
  • fat0,04 g
  • carbohydrates7,0 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 120,3 kJ (28,8 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 1 l
Cook Method
Defrost until completely thawed.
