Uzvar

Uzvar is a traditional Ukrainian drink from dried fruits and berries. TM «SmaCom» created a truly homemade, delicious uzvar to be indispensable on your holiday table. The drink is successfully combined with summer berries: apples, pears, rosehips.

Uzvar is a source of vitamins, because fruits only bringing to boil, and is not subjected to prolonged cooking. Be healthy with TM «SmaCom»!

Ingredients
Drinking water, dried fruits – 10% (apples, pears, wild rose), sugar, citric acid.
