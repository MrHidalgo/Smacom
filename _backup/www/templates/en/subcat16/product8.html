Creamy mushroom soup

Frozen pre-cooked meals of TM “SmaCom” are invented for those who value theirs time and Energy (caloric) 100 g. It’s enough simply to warm up the food in microwave oven and your dinner is ready. TM “SmaCom” offers really flavoured, nourishing, delicious first course – “creamy mushroom soup”.

Even the most exacting gourmands will not refuse from it. Those who never tried creamy mushroom soup of TM “SmaCom” lost a lot, because this wonderful dish is able to satisfy everybody.

Ingredients
Water, mushrooms, dried milk, potatoes, first grade wheat flour, margarine (refined deodorized fat and oil, emulsifier Е471 (Soya Lecithin, potassium sorbate, flavour enhance of milk and butter, citric acid, natural food colour additive carotene), salt, fresh onions, ground black pepper.

Cook Method

1. Briquette: defrost until completely thawed, cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into microwave oven and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.

2. Glass: cover and remove the protective foil in the microwave to warm up 7-10 minutes.
