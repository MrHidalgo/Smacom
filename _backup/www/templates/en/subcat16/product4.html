Vegetable soup

Want something simply but healthy? Vegetable soup of TM “SmaCom” is the best choice! Ready-made first meal is the pleasure for veggies and those who keep the diet and nutrition.

Vegetable soup is extremely flavoured and reminds summer, when it’s already not warm weather outside. Vegetables are the main ingredients in soup, which doesn’t give way to meat meals. Eat and enjoy together with TM “SmaCom”!

Ingredients
Water, potatoes, vegetable mixture, fresh onion, refined deodorized sunflower-seed oil, fresh carrot, salt.
Nutritional (food) information:
  • proteins0,8 g
  • fat0,9 g
  • carbohydrates4,0 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 114,22 kJ (27,3 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 0,3 kg
  • 1 kg
Cook Method

1. Briquette: defrost until completely thawed, cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into microwave oven and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.

2. Glass: cover and remove the protective foil in the microwave to warm up 7-10 minutes.
