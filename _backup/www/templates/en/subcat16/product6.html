Rice soup

Rice is not only rice porridge or pilaf but also very tasty soups. Rice soup with meat of TM “SmaCom” is delicious and nourishing. Moreover, rice soup does look wonderfully golden due to fried onions which enhance the flavor. Such meal will please even fussy foodies.

Rice soup is a kind of warming soups and will be the best choice in cold weather. It will be healthy to people who keep the balanced diet strictly and breast-feeding mothers. It follows that rice soup is allowed to serve for kids but nicely blended.

Ingredients
Water, potatoes, meat stock (chicken hip, shin) rice, fresh onions, refined deodorized sunflower-seed oil, fresh carrot, salt, ground black pepper.

  • proteins1,9 g

  • carbohydrates4,6 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 161,5 kJ (38,6 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 0,3 kg
  • 0,5 kg
Cook Method

Defrost until completely thawed, cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into microwave oven and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.
