Creamy beet borshch

Culinary world is so diverse. TM “SmaCom” doesn’t stop to surprise by its new and unique meals, they enhance interest to find out their taste. So the cooks invented traditional first course “Ukrainian borshch”, served in a different way and that changed the attitude to the dish completely.

Borshch has thicker texture than usual borshch, because of blending all ingredients so it even doesn’t need thickening agent. If you want to surprise your guests by your culinary innovations, creamy beet borshch of TM “SmaCom” is the right choice.

Ingredients
Water, potatoes – 10,7%, frozen meat stock – 9,8% (chicken breasts), cabbage, fresh onion, beet root, refined deodorized sunflower-seed oil, tomato paste, fresh carrot, salt, sugar, bell pepper, vinegar, ground black pepper.
Nutritional (food) information:
  • proteins2,8 g
  • fat2,6 g
  • carbohydrates4,1 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 213,38 kJ (51,0 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 0,25 kg
  • 0,3 kg
  • 1 kg
Cook Method

Defrost until completely thawed, cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into microwave oven and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.
