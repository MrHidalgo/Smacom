Creamy spinach soup

Elegant and simple creamy spinach soup is one of the innovation of French cuisine. French people call spinach as a king of vegetables as they use it in many dishes. These green leaves have high nutritional value.

TM “SmaCom” reinvented French culinary masterpiece for gourmands.

Elegant creamy spinach soup will be loved by passionate veggies. Those who tried spinach leaves know that they are tender and have slight taste.

But just put them into soup, when spinach opens its delicate taste. Even the most exacting gourmand will appreciate this wonderful dish. Serve Creamy spinach soup with croutons or toasts.

Ingredients
Water, frozen spinach – 25,5 %, dried milk, first grade wheat flour, margarine (refined deodorized fat and oil, emulsifier Е471 (Soya Lecithin, potassium sorbate, flavour enhance of milk and butter, citric acid, natural food colour additive carotene), salt, fresh onions, ground black pepper.

Cook Method

Defrost until completely thawed, cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into microwave oven and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.
