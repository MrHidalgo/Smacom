Cheeseburger with cutlet of turkey

TM «Smacom» created a new line of ready meals – Cheeseburgers, which have long been an attribute catering. Fragrant bun, which is covered with sesame seeds, chop meat, cheese, fresh tomato, pickles, onions, ketchup and mustard are combined with cheeseburgers «Smacom».

It is hard to imagine, but due to dietary meat turkey, we have created a truly healthy food, which will replace dinner.

2

Turkey meat gives the dish a delicate flavor and juiciness, the body - easy and satiety.

Cutlet in cheeseburger is the ready for use, fry it, cool and compose with other ingredients. Wrap frozen cheeseburger in the special branded paper and colorful cardboard box.

Cheeseburger only need to reheat in the microwave. Who does not have time for lunch break, try the cheeseburger! He will give us the energy and the mood for further work.

Ingredients
Cheeseburger bun: wheat, drinking water, margarine, milk, sugar, yeast pressed, salt.
Burger cheeseburger for: frozen raw meat - beef znezhylovane, drinking water, onion, salt, ground black pepper; cheese, pickled cucumber, mustard sauce, tomato sauce, onions, fresh tomatoes.
