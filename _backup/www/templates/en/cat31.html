Fried chebureki is a bright culinary trend which possesses the honorable place in Ukrainian cuisine. It differs thanks to its crispy deep-fried dough and juicy fragrant meat.

This is a national dish of the Crimean Tatars. The filling is ground beef, seasoned previously with aroma spices

The unique juiciness the dish gets from Ukrainian onions. The result - delicious half moons stuffed with tender ground beef.
