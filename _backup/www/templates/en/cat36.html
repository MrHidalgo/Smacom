Poltava region has always been hospitable and welcoming. And if Poltava galushki, dumplings and cabbage rolls have always remained a favorite delicacy among inhabitants and visitors of the region, we have somehow forgotten about the patties, but... the traditions are back now!

Sweet, fancy, patties with various fillings, baked according to the newest technologies and recipes with traditional homemade taste, are presented by SmaCom™. We offer baking with lots of fillings and a thin layer of fragrant, fancy airy dough.

Appraise the constant quality and variety of fillings. With meat, cabbage, potatoes, liver, jam or cherry... patties from SmaCom™ will highlight any occasion, whether it is corporate, wedding, birthday or any other event. We bake our patties under a specially developed recipe which will be appreciated by even the most sophisticated gourmets.
