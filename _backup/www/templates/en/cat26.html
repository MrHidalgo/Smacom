SmaCom™ meat dumplings are delicious, mouthwatering and elegant masterpieces of culinary art, their advantage is in thin pastry dough made of extra quality flour and in selected tender juicy meat filling.

Framed in a decorative sophisticated molding, cooked according to classic recipes, they dominate over gourmets around the world. SmaCom™ invites you to enjoy the best meat dumplings masterpieces of world cuisine, the primordial recipe of which is complied in the preparation.

All kinds of SmaCom meat dumplings are created in accordance with national traditions exclusively from selected natural ingredients. Meat dumplings are made according to the current state standards for the production of this product.
