Cabbage rolls

«Ukrainian»
1

"And make me your cabbage rolls, Mother" — the famous Ukrainian character said. He finally inspired Ukrainians in the belief that the "cabbage rolls" is their national dish. Neither a hearty breakfast nor a delicious lunch nor a rich dinner will be held without cabbage rolls.

Cabbage rolls are so firmly entrenched in Ukraine, that they became a national dish. However, this dish is of an eastern or rather Turkic origin. Probably, such a synthesis of two different culinary cultures has spawned so lovely, exquisite and versatile at the same time delicacy. Ukrainians, notable chefs, have brought their innovations to the Turkic colouring. Cabbage rolls are loved, tasty and healthy!

Cook Method

Mix tomato sauce or ketchup with sour cream and water (or broth). Pour EkoSmac™ cabbage rolls with this mixture so that the liquid barely cover them. Cook over medium heat until cooked. When serving pour the cabbage rolls with sour creme and sprinkle with chopped salad.
