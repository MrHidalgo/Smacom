Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the meat dumplings "Bogatyrskiye" into boiling salted water (1 part of meat dumplings to 4 parts of water). Bring to a boil, cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat until cooked. Serve the meat dumplings with grated garlic, mixed with oil, vinegar, salt and pepper.
