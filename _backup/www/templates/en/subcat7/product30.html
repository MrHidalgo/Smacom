Chebureki fried

The Ukrainians enjoy chebureki from Smacom for a long time. Before the chebureki was called as a fried pastry cases of lean dough and filled with hot spices, cheese or mushrooms. Nowadays, chebureki cooked of good fresh meat.

Taking into account that semi-finished food need some time to be cooked, we decided to offer a new format of meal – fried chebureki.

Ingredients
Flour 1/c, drinking water, egg powder, vegetable oil refined, salt, beef boiled, baked offal, onion, black pepper, fragrant pepper.

Cook Method

Chebureki fry in deep frying or in a pan in a large amount of vegetable oil, over medium heat from two sides to a ruddy crust.
