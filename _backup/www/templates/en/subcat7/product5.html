Dumplings

with meat and garlic
1

''You are welcome to all we have'' - so says a Russian proverb. Russians always had plenty of meat and garlic. These two products are considered essential and obligatory in the daily diet. Meat is the universal source of Energy (caloric) 100 g and vitamins. Garlic is the best defender against diseases. Their combination is a special song, an ode to the perfect culinary taste.

A classic Russian recipe is used in cooking EkoSmac dumplings. It's said that such dumplings were loved by Tsar Ivan the Terrible. However, these dumplings contain such Ukrainian ingredients: selected minced meat mixed with natural "snooty" garlic, which adds highly visible and rich taste.

Ingredients
Boiled beef, extra class flour, drinking water, browned onions, garlic, salt, egg powder, vegetable oil, black pepper.

Cook Method

Put the dumplings in a plenty of salted boiling water. Boil until cooked. Finished dumplings start to float. Remove them with a slotted spoon and put into a pan with a pre-fried onion. Fry until golden brown. Serve with garlic and sour cream.
