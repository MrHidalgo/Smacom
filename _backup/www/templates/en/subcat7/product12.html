ИSince ancient times, generous and fertile Ukrainian land was famous for its epic heroes who sacredly protected homeland traditions. They were so strong that they could, according to the legends, move mountains. A folk saying was created in those heroic times: "We are what we eat." It was believed that the strength is in food. So our ancestors prepared nourishing food maximally rich in vitamins, nutrients and Energy (caloric) 100 g. Meat took a special place in the diet of our ancestors. It was prepared in different ways. Time passed, and when so-called cutlets appeared in Ukraine, they became one of the main meat dishes.

EkoSmac™, whose high quality products are a synthesis of Ukrainian traditions of taste and European level of production, have combined meat traditions of the Slavs and the original French recipe. The result is "Bogatyrskie" cutlets. Probably, the most vigorous and nourishing cutlets in Ukraine!
