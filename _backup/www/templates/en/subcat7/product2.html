"Zastolnye"

meat dumplings
1

Gathering around the table has been a favorite tradition of the Slavs for a long time. Since ancient times, they have loved to gather at the festive table, crammed with a variety of delicacies. Meat dumplings, cooked by a special recipe, have held a central place for centuries. Meat dumplings were also perfectly combined with conversations.

SmaCom™ cooks have recreated this recipe by creating "Zastolnye'' meat dumplings. This is the best holidays and every day appetizer. Firstly, "Zastolnye" meat dumplings have a pleasant spicy flavor, which greatly shields alcoholic beverages, secondly, they are nourishing and juicy.

2

What is so special about the recipe? The filling is a harmonious combination of pork, chicken and onions mixed in certain proportions that were used at the court of Vladimir Monomakh. Pork gives a gentle satiety to meat dumplings, chicken gives airy lightness, and onions give juicy freshness.

SmaCom™ meat dumplings "Zastolnye'' are the best dish on the table. You can cook them in only 5 minutes, which means that guests will be fed, and you'll be satisfied. This is a real economy of time and Energy (caloric) 100 g! SmaCom™ meat dumplings "Zastolnye" - a holiday in 5 minutes!

Cook Method

Fill the well frozen dumplings "Zastolnye" in low-fat broth, flavored with spices (marjoram, pepper, etc.). After the preparation, carefully decant all the liquid. Meat dumplings are served on a plate with mayonnaise or sauce.
