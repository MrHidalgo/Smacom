EkoSmac™ proudly presents "Chicken Nuggets" — a largely unique product. This product is made from chicken breast, cut carefully along the fibers into oblong slices. Only this technology of cutting allows saving the maximum authentic taste of poultry. These pieces are crumbled in a special composition of the flour, seasoned with Indian spices, and then pre-fried with onions.

This recipe allows you to get the chicken fingers are cooked quickly and easily. On an output we have delicious, appetizing, fragrant chicken nuggets. And how nice, how stunning is the crisp, which is formed when the nuggets are cooked! It is piquant and fragrant! It will delight any gourmet! Natural spices and Ukrainian garlic which are added to the nuggets admire with its taste and tender birds meat simply melts in your mouth.
