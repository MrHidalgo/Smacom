Chevupeli is a good tandem of classic pelmeni and fried chebureki, a little crispy with tender juicy filling. They look like mini dumplings deep fried. You will be delighted with chevupeli with beef and pork on a picnic or at home.

They are filled with natural ground meat, filling per cent no less than 45%.

Chevupeli with cheese and ham is a great universal appetizer much of the time.

Tender processed cheese with fragrant ham is an excellent filling to ease hunger and to leave nobody different after this taste.

The cooking method is convenient and easy as well, just warm it up in a microwave oven around some minutes and the meal is ready.
