Dumplings “Firmovi”

with mushrooms

Product range of dumplings “Firmovi” of TM SmaCom was added with unique dish: dumplings with mushrooms. The thing of brand “Formovi” is that we use only natural meat, homemade cooking, unique form of product and eco craft packaging as well.

The filling consists of mushrooms 80% so that it has good mushroom taste. We added fried onions, salt and pepper as well. Dumplings with mushrooms are dietary healthy dish, it can be consumed both vegetarians and people who go on a fast.

Ingredients
Dough: top-grade flour, potable water, deodorized sunflower oil, edible table salt. Filling: mushrooms 80%, fried onions, edible table salt, milk powder, black ground pepper.
