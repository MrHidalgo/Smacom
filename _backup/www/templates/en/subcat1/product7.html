It is believed that above all culinary delights, Kiev is famous for cutlets and cakes. But there is another great dish, a favorite among all the people of Kiev and Ukrainians. It is "Kiev" meat dumplings.

SmaCom™ meat dumplings "Kiev" embody the true Kiev traditions of a light and delicate taste. As in "Kiev" cutlets, the main feature in the meat dumplings is tender poultry. In combination with finely chopped fresh herbs, spices and natural homemade butter, meat filling has an original, truly refined taste.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the meat dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of meat dumplings to 4 parts of water). Boil them until half-cooked, put in pots and pour with the hot broth with sour cream and fried mushrooms. Close the pots and cook in the oven until ready.
