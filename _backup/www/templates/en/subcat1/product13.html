Dumplings “Firmovi”

with tender turkey fillet

“SmaCom” has created a special line of dumplings by unique author's recipe. “Firmovi” is a product of high quality level, for this production we used only selective and natural ingredients.

We made sure that the consumer can easily select the product for his taste from a wide range. Particular attention should be paid to turkey meat dumplings, they are low-calorie, sweet, diet and healthy. Also dill gives the dumplings a special savory taste.

Let’s try the dumplings “Firmovi” with a tender turkey fillet and rate the quality on taste!

Ingredients
Dough: extra class flour, potable water, eggs, salt. Stuffing: frozen raw meat (turkey meat, chicken meat), fresh onion, water, margarine, salt, dill, white sugar.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the meat dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of meat dumplings to 4 parts of water). Boil them until half-cooked, put in pots and pour with the hot broth with sour cream. Close the pots and cook until ready in the oven. Strew with greens when serving.
