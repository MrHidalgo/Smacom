Dumplings “Firmovi”

from selected pork and flavored nutmeg

“Smacom” presents a new author recipe – dumplings “Firmovi” from selected pork and flavored nutmeg. “Firmovi” is a product of high quality level, for this production we used only selective and natural ingredients.

These dumplings are characterized by a special taste and aroma due to spicy nutmeg. Nutmeg is a very well known spice in the world, it’s widely used in cooking to achieve the perfect taste. Nutmeg with intensely spicy aroma and sweet-bitter taste gives the meat delicacy and piquancy.

Dumplings from selected pork and nutmeg can be considered a perfect taste.

Ingredients
Dough: wheat flour / s, potable water, eggs, salt. Minced meat: raw frozen meat (pork tendon removed, chicken meat), fresh onion, water, salt, nutmeg.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the meat dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of meat dumplings to 4 parts of water). Boil them until half-cooked, put in pots and pour with the hot broth with sour cream. Close the pots and cook until ready in the oven. Strew with greens when serving.
