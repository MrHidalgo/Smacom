Cook Method

Well frozen dumplings "Siberian" to fill in the low-fat broth, seasoned with spices (marjoram, pepper and others). After cooking all the liquid carefully decant (serve dumplings in broth Siberians considered mauves ton). Dumplings serve on a platter with a special sauce. To prepare the sauce you want on a dry hot pan fry the flour to brown, then dilute it with meat broth, put the butter, add to taste ground black pepper and salt. Separately to the dumplings to serve the vinegar and broth.
