Smacom™ frozen pre-cooked meal is a full, natural, homemade tasty food for people who care about their health and appreciate their time.

It is simply and quickly to cook frozen pre-cooked meals. Just warm them up for about 5-7 minutes. The production technology is systematically balanced. It includes all steps of preparing a finished meal. We use only natural quality ingredients and it gives special palatability to the product. After complete cooling, the finished product enters the shock freezing cabinets.

Frozen pre-cooked meals do not contain soybeans, food grade dyes and preservatives and are safe for health. Smacom™ offers frozen pre-cooked meals in assortment: Uzbek pilaf, noodles with meat sauce, chicken cutlets with mushroom sauce with mashed potatoes, baked pork with potato wedges and chicken meat with tomato sauce with buckwheat. Frozen pre-cooked meals are quickly, tasty and satisfying!
