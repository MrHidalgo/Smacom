Mashed potatoes

Mashed potatoes – is one of the most popular side dishes in our country. It goes well with any meat, vegetables, fish and leaves the feeling of fullness for a long time.

But to open its flavor mashed potatoes should be cooked properly. TM «SmaCom» cooks offers frozen pre-cooked mashed potatoes, tender and delicate as well.

Ingredients
Boiled potatoes – 81 %, water, margarine, salt.
Nutritional (food) information:
  • proteins3,2 g
  • fat4,0 g
  • carbohydrates27,5 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 664,42 kJ (158,8 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 1 kg
Cook Method

Product should be pre-defrosted. Cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into the oven and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.
