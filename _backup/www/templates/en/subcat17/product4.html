Ingredients
Water, fresh pepper, frozen meat stock – 17,3 % (chicken stock – 13,2 %, beef stock – 2,7 %, fat – 1,4 %), fried onions, tomato paste, carrots, rice, refined deodorized sunflower-seed oil, salt, textured soy protein (TSP), beans, ground black pepper.

Cook Method

Patties do not need to be pre-defrosted. Put the frozen pepper in a frying pan, add a little amount of water and stew at a very low heat under the closed lid for 5 – 7 minutes, turn on both sides. Preparing on a cooking basket: don’t defrost the pepper, insert the basket with pepper into a large pan containing boiling water and cover firmly. When the water starts boiling, cook for about 5 minutes. The steamer can be used as well. In a microwave oven: place frozen pepper into the oven and warm up for 4 – 7 minutes at the power of 700 watts. The heating time depends on the quantity of products simultaneously warmed up: large number of patties require more time.
