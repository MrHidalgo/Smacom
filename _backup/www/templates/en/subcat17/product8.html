Boiled with mushrooms

TM «SmaCom» offers frozen pre-cooked dish, just warm it up. Even those who are not fond of buckwheat, couldn’t stand not to try. Fried pieces of mushrooms give unique flavor and taste. Innovative frozen system gives the opportunity to preserve nutrients, flavor and aromatic properties as well.

Crumbly buckwheat with tender mushrooms is a light variant of perfect breakfast and excellent second course in a dinner time.

Ingredients
Water, buckwheat – 25 %, mushrooms – 15%, refined deodorized sunflower-seed oil, salt.
Nutritional (food) information:
  • proteins1,2 g
  • fat5,5 g
  • carbohydrates1,0 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 244,0 kJ (58,3 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 1 kg
Cook Method

Product should be pre-defrosted. Cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into the oven and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.
