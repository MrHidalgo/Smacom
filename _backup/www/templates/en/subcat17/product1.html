Braised cabbage

Braised cabbage with meat is rich separate second course that doesn't need side orders. The cabbage cut into long thin strips is very soft, lush and meat is delicate and tasty as well. Due to harmonized combination of selected vegetables, carrots, onions and salt, sugar, pepper, tomato paste and butter as well, cabbage and chicken breasts open their unique taste.

Besides unique taste the meal is very nourishing as the cabbage is a source of nutritive minerals. As the cabbage is low in calories and high in fiber, it is a valuable dietary food. Those who adore the braised cabbage will assess the delicious offer from TM “SmaCom”

Ingredients
Cabbage – 74%, chicken breasts – 10%, carrots, carrots, fried onion, tomato paste, refined deodorized sunflower seed oil, salt, sugar, water, ground black pepper, first grade wheat flour.
