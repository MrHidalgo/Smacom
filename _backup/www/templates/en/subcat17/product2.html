Braised cabbage with mushrooms

Cabbage became one of the favorite crops since the beginning of time as it is a good source of vitamins and goes well with any food.

So ТМ «SmaCom» offers delicious and nutritious meal “Braised cabbage with mushrooms”.

Such meal will satisfy anybody, especially who keeps up the balanced diet strictly. Despite the fact that mushrooms are low in calories, they provide important nutrients, vitamins and proteins.

Those who will not refuse from juicy, tender meat chop, cabbage with mushrooms is excellent side order. Following ordinary cooking technology braised cabbage with mushrooms come out really homemade dish.

Ingredients
Cabbage – 74%, fried mushrooms, carrots, fried onion, tomato paste, refined deodorized sunflower seed oil, salt, water, ground black pepper, first grade wheat flour.
Nutritional (food) information:
  • proteins4,0 g
  • fat1,0 g
  • carbohydrates5,7 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 200,0 kJ (47,8 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 1 kg
Cook Method
Cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into microwave oven in a packing and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.
