Rice with vegetables

TM «SmaCom» offers pre-cooked meal, which just need to be warmed up. Boiled rice and vegetables – are the main ingredients of this meal, which is not only delicious and flavored, but provide a source of many nutrients. Vegetables are the source of rich minerals and vitamins. Cooked vegetables preserve all vitamins. They are easy to digest and gentle on your stomach.

That’s why don’t forget about rice cereal with vegetable on your breakfast, even if you have already grew up.

Ingredients
Boiled rice – 67 %, vegetable mix – 29 %, refined deodorized sunflower-seed oil, salt.
Nutritional (food) information:
  • proteins5,6 g
  • fat9,9 g
  • carbohydrates51,3 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 1325,1 kJ (316,7 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 1 kg
Cook Method

Product should be pre-defrosted. Cut the packing a little bit, place the dish into the oven and warm up for 7 – 10 minutes at the power of 750 watts.
