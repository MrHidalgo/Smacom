Mini belyashi

All get used to see belyashi of big size, that's why mini belyashi from TM “SmaCom” will surprise everybody of theirs uniqueness. Small fried meat pies will be good beer appetizers.

They are very delicious and appetizing, satisfy your hunger and characterized with its flavor and tenderness. Belyashi are not only delicious but have aesthetic appearance with gold crispy skin.

Ingredients
First grade wheat flour, water, refined deodorized sunflower-seed oil, baking yeast, sugar, salt. Minced meat: onions, frozen meat stock – 40% (mechanically deboned meat (MDM) – 10%, beef – 10%, pork – 10%, lard – 10%).

  • proteins6,3 g
  • fat8,2 g
  • carbohydrates33,0 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 966,5 kJ (231,0 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 0,4 kg
Cook Method
Place the dish into the oven and warm up for 5 – 7 minutes at the power of 700 watts.
