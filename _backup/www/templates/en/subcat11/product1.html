Cabbage rolls with meat cooking frozen

1

SmaCom presents the homemade cabbage rolls in the lineup of finished dishes. They are so entrenched in the Ukraine, which have become a national dish. From the historical viewpoint cabbage rolls could arise not as a food, for the purpose of to preserve food. Our ancestors used large leaves of plants (cabbage, grapes and even algae), to turn their food and keep it longer.

Cook Method

In the microwave: Place the frozen dish in the microwave in the tray with slightly ajar film and warm up at a power of 700 watts for 5-7 minutes. Warm up preliminary unfrozen dish for 3-4 minutes. Serve hot.

In the oven: Preheat oven at a temperature of 200 C. Place the frozen dish in the tray without film and warm up in the oven for 25 -30 minutes. Warm up preliminary defrost dish for 10-15 minutes.
