Considering the popularity of these products Smacom™ experts recreated the taste of this dish at home traditions of the Ukrainian cuisine. It should be said that it is extraordinarily healthy product. In fact poultry and buckwheat contain the variety of useful vitamins and minerals. It’s a great tandem of dietary satiety.

Ingredients
Buckwheat porridge 170 g: buckwheat, sunflower oil, salt. Fillet 100 g: chicken fillet, sunflower oil, salt. Sauce 80 g.: sunflower oil, extra class wheat flour, tomato puree, water, sugar, salt, milled black pepper.

Cook Method
Take off cardboard packing. Lift the protective film or pierce in several places. Put the tray in the microwave or oven. Microwave at a power of 700 watts for 5-7 minutes, warm it up in the oven at 130-150 degrees for 30 minutes.
