Fish dumplings

with salmon and pink salmon

Unusual and incredibly delicate dumplings with salmon and pink salmon handmade by SmaCom™ were created exclusively for gourmets and lovers of an extraordinary and refined taste.

Unusual filling of chilled fillets of salmon and pink salmon is used in these meat dumplings. This industrial fish of the salmon family is useful at all indicators due to its high nutritional value. A real health formula is hidden in the fish products: easily assimilated proteins, fatty acids, vitamin D and a number of minerals such as iodine, selenium, fluorine, magnesium, calcium. Fish is processed by all technologies in compliance with quality standards, which ensures maximum of the nutritional value. Adding marjoram to the mince makes its own "flavor" and has a unique taste. It is not only tasty, but also healthy.

Eat to your health!

Ingredients
Extra class flour, fish fillets (salmon, pink salmon), water, onion, eggs, salt, sunflower oil, marjoram, black pepper.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the meat dumplings with salmon and pink salmon into boiling salted water (1 part of meat dumplings to 4 parts of water), add bay leaf. Bring to a boil, cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat until cooked. Remove them with a slotted spoon and put them on a large plate. Spice up to your taste.
