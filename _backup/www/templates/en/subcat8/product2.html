Meat dumplings

with beef and pork

Rich taste of beef and pork combined with natural spices - these are premium SmaCom™ meat dumplings.

Meat Dumplings with beef and pork are an exclusive handmade product. Palatability speaks for itself, because only selected natural ingredients are used for the filling while cooking the meat dumplings - ecologically friendly chilled beef and pork. Pieces of lard add great juiciness, and black pepper - sharpness and national flavor. Delicate and thin dough of the highest quality flour and hand-figured molding provide excellent aesthetics and exceptional palatability of the product. Meat Dumplings with beef and pork is a versatile, nourishing and tasty dish.

Enjoy your meal with SmaCom!

Ingredients
Extra class flour, beef, pork meat, lard, water, onion, eggs, salt, sunflower oil, black pepper.

Cook Method

Without defrosting, put the meat dumplings into boiling salted water (1 part of meat dumplings to 4 parts of water). Bring to a boil, cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat until cooked. Remove them with a slotted spoon and put them on a large plate. Spice up to your taste.
