Generous Ukrainian cuisine is unimaginable without dumplings. SmaCom™ offers premium hand-made products - dumplings with cherries.

In Ukraine, this culinary masterpiece has become popular thanks to the Turks: an eastern dish "dyush-vara" first became "varAniks" and then "vareniks" (a Ukrainian word for dumplings). Today, there are thousands of dumplings recipes in the arsenal of the Ukrainian cuisine, but adherents of this dish will surely say that the real dumplings should be sweet.

Cook Method

Put cherry dumplings in a plenty of salted boiling water. Boil until cooked. Finished dumplings start to float. Remove them with a slotted spoon and pour the warmed butter or sour cream on them. Sweet syrup can be served separately. The best syrup choice is cherry or black cherry syrup, you can also add jam.
