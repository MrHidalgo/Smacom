Grechaniky

A marvelous combination of juicy pork, vitamin beef and tender poultry are used in grechaniky. These are the three kinds of meat from which grechaniky has always been prepared in Ukraine. It should be said that it is a surprisingly healthy dish. Meat and buckwheat contain a variety of vitamins and minerals. It is a real treasury of protein, iron, B vitamins, and much more.

Innovative forming equipment gives grechaniky their perfect appetizing round shape and gives a rich pleasant aroma. Cooked grechaniky can be served with sour cream. Grechaniky from Smacom are the delicious traditions of Ukraine!

Ingredients
Beef, pork, poultry meat, buckwheat, salt.
