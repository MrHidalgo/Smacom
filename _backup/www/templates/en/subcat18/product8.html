"Bogatyrskie" cutlets

Smacom™, whose high quality products are a synthesis of Ukrainian traditions of taste and European level of production, have combined meat traditions of the Slavs and the original French recipe. The result is "Bogatyrskie" cutlets. Probably, the most vigorous and nourishing cutlets in Ukraine!

Beef, pork, water, mid-back fat, onion, soy protein, semolina, bread crumbs, salt, black pepper, garlic.

Cook Method

The steamer can be used as well. In a microwave oven: place frozen pepper into the oven and warm up for 3 – 5 minutes at the power of 700 watts. The heating time depends on the quantity of products simultaneously warmed up: large number of patties require more time for warming up.
