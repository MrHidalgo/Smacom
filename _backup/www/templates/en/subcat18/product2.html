Meatballs in sauce

TM «SmaCom» offers an excellent alternative to cutlets – meatballs. From cutlets they are distinguished by a few culinary attributes: firstly, the round shape, and secondly, they are coated only in flour, and third, they are served with the sauce together with a dish.

This feature in the origin of meatballs, which came to us from the national cuisine of the Turkic peoples across the Balkans and Austrian cuisine. Where they were first invented, the meatballs were served on a platter in thick gravy. Meat balls, cooked in a sauce is a popular dish in diet cuisine, they are perfectly combined with any customary garnish.

Ingredients
Raw meat, frozen – 42% (trimmed pork (thigh) – 15%, fat 14%, trimmed beef – 13%), boiled rice, fresh onion, water, bread, salt, ground black pepper.

Cook Method

Patties do not need to be pre-defrosted. Steaming: Put the meatballs cooking basket pan, insert the basket into a large pan containing boiling water and cover firmly. When the water start boiling, cook for about 8 – 10 minutes. The steamer can be used as well. In a microwave oven: place frozen pepper into the oven and warm up for 3 – 5 minutes at the power of 700 watts. The heating time depends on the quantity of products simultaneously warmed up: large number of patties require more time for warming up.
