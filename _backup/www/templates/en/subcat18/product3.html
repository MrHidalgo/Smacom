Cutlets

«Homemade»

Cutlets "Home" – a symbol of love, care and family comfort. Where people enjoy cutlets of TM «SmaCom», reigns joy and homely atmosphere.

Recipe for meatballs established in accordance with all standards.

The unique and delicate taste of homemade cutlets felt thanks to the right combination of pork and chicken meat, which requires a special approach and knowledge of many culinary subtleties.

Cutlets can be served with greens, vegetables, any side dish, and your favourite sauce will enhance their home taste.

Ingredients
Raw meat, frozen – 65% (pork trimmed – 30%, chicken meat (thighs) – 25%, fat – 10%), white bread, fresh onion, water, salt, ground black pepper, dried garlic.
Nutritional (food) information:
  • proteins11,5 g
  • fat10,3 g
  • carbohydrates1,3 g
Energy (caloric) 100 g:
  • 978,6 kJ (233,9 kcal)
Net weight and packing:
  • 1 kg
Cook Method

Grease the pan with butter or sunflower oil. Heat it. Lay out the cutlents on a heated pan. Fry until brown for at least 10 minutes.
